TOXISCAN

The invention relates to ToxiScan, a real-time chemical detection system for wastewater from textile industries. It integrates sensors to measure pH, TDS, turbidity, conductivity, ammonia, and dye levels. A microcontroller processes the sensor data to determine pollutant concentrations. The system uses an IoT module to enable remote monitoring and data logging. Alerts are generated when chemical levels exceed safe thresholds. The device is portable, low-cost, and easy to deploy. It provides continuous monitoring to ensure environmental compliance. Overall, ToxiScan promotes efficient wastewater management and environmental safety.

SMART-AGRO GREENHOUSE (PRESENT)

The objective is to contribute to the digitalization of the agricultural sector by implementing a cloud-based solution for greenhouse crop production. This approach provides services for economical and environmental benefits, improving system efficiency through suggestions for water, pesticide, fertigation, and energy use. It enables access to historical and real-time data, supports adding new devices globally, offers 48-hour weather forecasts, and integrates GMaaS for estimating indoor climate, crop production, and irrigation values via REST API.
